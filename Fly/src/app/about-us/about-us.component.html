About FlySky

FlySky is India’s largest online flight ticket booking platform. that has transformed flight travel in the country by bringing ease and convenience to millions of Indians who travel using flights. Founded in 2019, FlySky is part of India’s leading online travel company. By providing widest choice, superior customer service, lowest prices and unmatched benefits, FlySky has served over 2 million customers. FlySky has a global presence with operations across Indonesia, Singapore and Malaysia apart from India.
